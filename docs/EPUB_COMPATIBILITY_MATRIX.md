Current engineering target is reflowable EPUB. Real commercial EPUB compatibility must be tested by users with their own legally obtained files and should not be committed to this repository.

When reporting compatibility issues for `v0.2.5-alpha-rc`, provide validation reports, external EPUBCheck output, diagnostic bundles, and the smallest copyright-safe fixture possible. Do not upload commercial EPUB files or exported translated EPUB files.
